Credit and Market Risk Aggregation under Basel III

In this project, we aim to quantify and aggregate both credit and market risk for a diversified portfolio under Basel III and IFRS 9 standards. We simulate Expected Credit Loss (ECL) using the Home Credit dataset, compute market risk using Value at Risk (VaR) and Conditional Value at Risk (CVaR), and apply macro-level stress testing with the St. Louis Fed Financial Stress Index. The goal is to understand portfolio-level risk under normal and stressed market conditions and visualize the impact across different scenarios.

Pricing American Options with Q Learning

In this project, we aim to learn an optimal pricing policy Q* for American options to maximize the expected cumulative reward over time. We estimate the continuation value using Laguerre Basis functions and compare it to the return of exercising the option.
